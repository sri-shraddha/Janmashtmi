Books for your well being to positive your soul and refresh your mind.

  • The Story of the Divine Flute-Player:

    This is a children's book that tells the story of Krishna's life in a simple and engaging way. It is a great way to introduce young children to Krishna.

  • The Mahabharata:

    This is an epic poem that tells the story of the Kurukshetra war, in which Krishna plays a pivotal role. The Mahabharata is a complex and fascinating work that explores themes of war, peace, love, and loss.

  • The Bhagavad Gita:

    This is the most important text associated with Krishna. It is a philosophical and theological dialogue between Krishna and the warrior prince Arjuna on the battlefield. The Gita explores a wide range of topics, including dharma (righteousness), karma (action), and reincarnation.
